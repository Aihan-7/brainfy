Flashcards for Nursing Students

Nursing school is a firehose of content on top of a brutal clinical schedule. Brainfy helps you turn your own notes and PDFs into spaced-repetition flashcards, build drug and dosage decks, and protect your study time with a planner and Pomodoro timer.

Open Brainfy → All study resources

Turn lectures and clinical notes into cards

Paste your lecture notes, upload a PDF, or photograph a textbook page, and Brainfy's AI drafts flashcards from it. The cards come from your material, so they line up with your program rather than a generic bank. You can also import existing Anki or Quizlet decks and browse community-shared sets from other nursing students. Brainfy does not ship verified expert decks, so always check clinical facts against your source.

Build drug and dosage decks you can trust

Pharmacology is where many nursing students lose points. Build your own drug decks from a class drug list or a lecture PDF, and use cloze cards to drill mechanisms, side effects, and dosage math. Because you build and edit every card, you control exactly what you are memorising. Verify doses and values against your source before relying on them.

How to build your nursing deck

1

Gather a source

Lecture notes, a chapter PDF, a drug list, or a photo of your textbook all work through one import.

2

Generate and edit

The AI drafts cards grounded in your text; you review and fix anything before it enters the deck.

3

Keep separate decks for pharm, med-surg, fundamentals, and labs so reviews stay focused.

4

Study on a schedule

Spaced repetition surfaces weak cards first; the planner and Pomodoro timer fit study around clinicals.

Plan study around clinical weeks

Clinical days, lecture, and skills labs leave study time scattered. Brainfy's weekly planner lets you block the gaps you actually have, and the Pomodoro timer turns a free hour into focused review without burning out. Streaks give you a gentle nudge to keep showing up, even on long shift days.

Frequently asked questions

Do I have to make my own cards?

You can do all three: let the AI draft cards from your notes or a PDF, write cards by hand, or import existing Anki and Quizlet decks. You can also browse community-shared sets. There is no pre-made verified nursing bank.

Can it read a photo of my textbook?

Yes. OCR reads photographed pages and diagrams, then the AI drafts cards from the extracted text, which you can edit before studying.

Is this good for pharmacology?

Yes. Build drug decks from a class list or lecture PDF and use cloze cards for mechanisms and doses. Always verify clinical values against your source.

How does the planner help with clinicals?

The weekly planner lets you block study into the gaps around clinical days and labs, and the Pomodoro timer keeps short sessions focused so scattered free time still counts.

Is Brainfy free for students?

Yes, free during beta with no ads, no deck limits, and a privacy-first design.
