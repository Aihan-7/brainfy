Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 9, 2026

Brainfy ("we", "us", or "our") operates the Brainfy study application available at brainfy.online. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our service.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you create an account or use Brainfy:

  • Account information: Your name and email address, provided via Google Sign-In or email/password registration.
  • Study data: Subjects, notes, uploaded documents, flashcards, and learning progress you create within the app.
  • Usage data: Basic information about how you interact with the app (e.g., features used), collected anonymously.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the Brainfy service.
  • To save and sync your study data across devices.
  • To generate AI-powered flashcards and summaries from your content.
  • To send you important service-related communications.

3. Data Storage

Your account and study data are stored securely using Google Firebase (Firestore). We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

4. AI Processing

When you use AI features (flashcard generation, summarisation), the content you submit is sent to a third-party AI provider (Groq or Anthropic) solely to generate the requested output. We do not use your content to train AI models.

5. Third-Party Services

Brainfy uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Firebase — authentication and data storage
  • Groq / Anthropic — AI text generation
  • Google Fonts — font delivery

6. Data Retention

We retain your data as long as your account is active. You may delete your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us at the email below.

7. Children's Privacy

Brainfy is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by posting the new policy on this page with an updated date.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: aihan@mifthas.com
