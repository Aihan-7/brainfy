Psychology Flashcards App

Intro psych is mostly vocabulary: theorists, terms, studies, and effects. Brainfy turns your textbook chapters and notes into spaced-repetition flashcards, so AP and college psych students can drill the terms with Learn and Test modes instead of rereading.

Open Brainfy → All study resources

Turn psych chapters into flashcards

Psychology rewards knowing definitions cold. Paste a chapter, upload a PDF, or photograph your notes, and Brainfy's AI drafts term-and-definition cards from your material. The cards match your course because they come from your source, not a generic set. You can also import existing Anki or Quizlet decks and browse community-shared sets. Brainfy does not ship verified psych decks, so check definitions and study details against your textbook.

Learn and Test modes for terms

Vocabulary-heavy subjects benefit from variety. Brainfy's Learn mode eases you into new terms, while Test mode quizzes you under exam-like pressure. Match mode pairs terms to definitions, and Cloze hides a key word inside a definition so you recall it directly. Rotating modes keeps long term-drilling sessions from going flat.

For intro, AP, and college psych

The workflow scales from a high-school AP Psychology unit to a college intro course: convert your reading into cards and let spaced repetition decide what to review each day. Add the Pomodoro timer and weekly planner to keep a steady pace through exam season.

Frequently asked questions

Can I build cards from a psychology textbook chapter?

Yes. Paste the text, upload a PDF, or photograph the pages, and the AI drafts term-and-definition cards. OCR handles photographed pages. Every card is editable.

Does Brainfy come with ready-made psych decks?

No. You build your own from your material, import existing Anki or Quizlet decks, or browse community-shared sets. Always verify definitions and study details against your textbook.

Which modes work best for psychology terms?

Learn and Test build recall, Match pairs terms to definitions, and Cloze hides a key word inside a definition. Rotating modes keeps vocabulary drilling fresh.

Is this good for AP Psychology?

Yes. The same workflow fits AP, intro, and college psych: turn your reading into cards and let spaced repetition schedule the reviews.

Is it free?

Yes, free during beta with no ads and no deck limits.
