Three ways to build a deck

Most flashcard tools make you type every card. Brainfy gives you three on-ramps so you spend your time studying, not building:

  • By hand — add cards one at a time with a fast editor when you want full control.
  • By import — paste or upload CSV, tab-separated, Anki "Notes in Plain Text", or a Quizlet export, and it becomes a deck in seconds.
  • With AI — paste notes or drop in a PDF or photo, and the AI drafts question-answer cards for you to keep or edit.

Frequently asked questions

Is the flashcard maker really free?

Yes — free during beta, with no ads and no paywall on the core maker, study modes, or spaced repetition. You can try it with no signup.

Do I have to type every card?

No. You can type cards by hand, import a CSV / tab-separated / Anki / Quizlet list, or let the AI draft a deck from pasted notes, a PDF, or a photo.

Can I edit AI-generated cards?

Yes — every AI-drafted card is fully editable before it enters your deck, so you keep what is right and fix or delete the rest.

Can I export my flashcards?

Yes — every deck exports to CSV, which re-imports into Anki, Quizlet, or a spreadsheet. No lock-in.

Is there a limit on how many cards I can make?

No deck caps during beta. Build as many cards and decks as you need.
