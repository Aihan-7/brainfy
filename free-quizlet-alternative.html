Frequently asked questions

Is Brainfy free with no ads?

Yes — free during beta and ad-free. The study modes and spaced repetition aren’t paywalled.

Can I import my Quizlet sets?

Yes. Use Quizlet’s Export, copy the text, and paste it into a deck’s Cards tab — Brainfy auto-detects the separator. CSV and tab-separated work too.

Does it have Learn, Test, and Match?

Yes — all of them, from any deck, plus classic flip review with spaced repetition.

What does Brainfy add that Quizlet doesn’t?

Real spaced-repetition scheduling, AI deck generation from notes/PDFs, a built-in focus timer and weekly planner, and a focus-aware study coach — all free.
