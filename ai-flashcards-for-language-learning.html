AI Flashcards for Language Learning

Vocabulary is a spaced-repetition problem — the right word at the right interval. Brainfy turns word lists, notes, and readings into review-ready flashcards and schedules them so new vocabulary actually sticks.

Open Brainfy → All study resources

Why SRS owns vocabulary

You don’t need to see “house → casa” every day forever — you need it exactly when it’s about to slip. Spaced repetition spaces those reviews out automatically, so 20 minutes a day compounds into a large, durable vocabulary.

Build a deck in seconds

  • Paste a word list as term, translation per line and import it straight into a deck.
  • Drop in reading notes and let the AI pull out key vocabulary and phrases.
  • Import from Quizlet or Anki — bring decks you already have, no retyping.

Then make it stick

Pair a short daily review with the focus timer, keep a gentle streak going, and let the multiple study modes (multiple choice, type-the-answer, match) keep recall sharp from more than one angle.

Frequently asked questions

Can I import a vocabulary list?

Yes — paste or upload a CSV / tab-separated list (term, translation) and it imports straight into a deck. Quizlet and Anki exports work too.

Does it support multiple study modes?

Yes: classic flip + spaced repetition, multiple-choice “Learn”, type-the-answer “Test”, and a timed “Match” game — all from the same deck.

Will it handle accents and other scripts?

Yes — card text is full Unicode, and answer checking is accent-aware so typed answers are graded fairly.

Is it free?

Yes, free during beta.
