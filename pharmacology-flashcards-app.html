Pharmacology Flashcards App

Drug classes, mechanisms, side effects, and doses pile up fast. Brainfy turns your drug lists and lecture PDFs into spaced-repetition flashcards and makes it easy to drill them with cloze deletions, the format pharm was made for.

Cloze deletions are perfect for pharm

Pharmacology is full of structured facts: a drug name, its class, its mechanism, its big side effect, its dose. Cloze (fill-in-the-blank) cards let you hide any one of those and recall it directly. Hide the mechanism and recall it from the drug name, or hide the drug from the class. That active recall is far stronger than rereading a drug table. Brainfy supports cloze alongside basic cards in every deck.

Build decks from drug lists or lecture PDFs

Paste a class drug list, upload a lecture PDF, or photograph a page, and the AI drafts cards grounded in your material rather than a generic set. You can also import existing Anki or Quizlet pharm decks and browse community-shared sets. Brainfy does not ship verified drug content, so always confirm mechanisms, interactions, and doses against your source and curriculum.

Frequently asked questions

Why is cloze so good for pharmacology?

Pharm facts are structured, so hiding one piece (a mechanism, a dose, a class) and recalling it forces active recall. That beats rereading a drug table. Brainfy supports cloze in any deck.

Can I build cards from a drug list?

Yes. Paste a list as drug, class, mechanism per line, or upload a lecture PDF or photo, and the AI drafts cards you can edit before studying.

Are the AI-generated drug facts verified?

No. The AI extracts from your source rather than inventing facts, but Brainfy does not provide verified drug content. Always confirm mechanisms, interactions, and doses against your source.

Can I import an existing pharm deck?

Yes. Import CSV, tab-separated, Anki plain-text, or Quizlet exports, and export your Brainfy decks back to CSV anytime. No lock-in.

Is it free?

Yes, free during beta with no ads and no deck limits.
