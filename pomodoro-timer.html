Frequently asked questions

Is the Brainfy Pomodoro timer really free?

Yes. Brainfy is free during beta — the Pomodoro timer and every other feature are unlocked at no cost, with no ads or paywalls.

Do I need to sign up to use the timer?

You can start a session immediately. Signing up only matters if you want your focus sessions to sync across devices and feed your study analytics.

Can I change the 25/5 intervals?

Yes — Brainfy ships the classic 25/5 preset, a longer deep-work preset, and a custom option for any session length.

Does it work on mobile?

Yes. Brainfy is web-based and runs in any modern mobile browser — no app install required.

What is the Pomodoro technique?

A focus method developed by Francesco Cirillo: work in short timed sprints (classically 25 minutes), separated by short breaks. It trains sustained attention and lowers decision fatigue.
